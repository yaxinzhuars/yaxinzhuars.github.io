About

Hi. I’m a first year PhD student at UMass Amherst, working with Prof. Hamed Zamani. Though interested in various topics, My previous experience is mainly related to knowledge graphs and cross-lingual tasks. Before Umass, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Gerard de Melo and Prof. Yongfeng Zhang during my master’s study at Rutgers University. I spent my undergraduate years at Nankai University.

Concact me at: yaxinzhu@cs.umass.edu
